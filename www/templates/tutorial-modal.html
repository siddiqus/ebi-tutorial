HOW TO PLAY

Welcome to the Equivalence Based Instruction Program Tutorial!
The objective of this program is to learn new material through picture associations. You will be presented with a series of multiple choice questions for Training and corresponding Tests. You may not initially know the answers, but you will be expected to learn them through trial and error.
Questions have a form similar to the one shown below:



The top image is the 'question'
Answer choices are on the bottom
For Training questions, tapping on an answer gives you feedback
Test questions individually will not have any feedback, but you will receive a Pass/Fail notice at the end of the test. You must answer 90% of the Test questions correctly to move forward. Tap on the button below to begin a demo of the program.
